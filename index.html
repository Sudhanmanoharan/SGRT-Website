About Us

  SGRT Facility provides the glazing industry, the construction sector and academic institutions with complete testing facilities for determining, understanding and analyzing the structural characteristics of Glass and Glazing products.

Future Goals

Education

▪ Vision to create more engineering minds in the areas of glass façade engineering.
▪ Initiating paper presentations and mini projects for the B Tech and M Tech civil students on architectural glass and glazing systems.
▪ Organizing training programs for the B Tech and M Tech civil students to learn about the different types of glass buildings and its materials.

Research

▪ Supporting Bureau of Indian Standards in building code of practices for the use of structural glazing in the country through continuous research.
▪ Encouraging passionate PhD scholars ad M Tech students in the research of glass facade engineering.

Testing

▪ Introducing the Energy performance (Optical and Simulation) testing of Architectural Glass at SGRT with the support of Government, Glazing Society of India and its Industry members and partners.
▪ Augmenting the facility with the capacity for the testing of profiles (aluminium and uPVC) with the support of Glazing Society of India and its industry members and partners.
▪ Understanding the testing of the other allied materials of glazing such as structural sealants, interlayers, gaskets etc.
