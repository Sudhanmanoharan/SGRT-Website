Testing Facilities: With the increased awareness towards testing of architectural glass for safety and structural performance among builders, architects, consultants, end users and all other stakeholders in the construction value chain, the Structural Glass Research and Testing (SGRT) Facility has become the prime center for testing and research on architectural glass. The SGRT facility has provided valuable inputs for the formulation of new standards for processed glass by the Bureau of Indian Standards.

Structural Glass Research and Testing (SGRT) Facility, a joint initiative of Indian Institute of Technology (IIT-M) and Glazing Society of India (GSI) is an advanced, first of its kind facility established at the Structural Engineering laboratory, Department of Civil Engineering in IIT Madras, Chennai, India.
